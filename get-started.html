Chat Engine

Use Hugging Face or OpenAI API.

HF

Hugging Face

Best when you want open models, provider flexibility, or your own inference endpoint.

  • Can run through Hugging Face Inference Providers or dedicated endpoints.
  • Good for experimenting with open roleplay/story models.
  • Needs more tuning, safety work, and latency testing.
AI

OpenAI API

Best for a smoother first chat launch with strong managed models and simpler operations.

  • Use your backend to call the API; never expose your key in the browser.
  • Works well with credit limits, daily caps, and conversation summaries.
  • Usually faster to ship than managing open-model hosting yourself.

Recommended path

Vercel or Cloudflare for the site, Supabase for accounts and profiles, OpenAI first for chat.
